Why secure your smart home?

Smart homes are popular

Smart Home devices certainly are a great addition to your life, they can make small tasks effortless and convenient.

They can serve as alarm clocks, radio/music streamers, thermostats and add features to applicances such as fridges. As well as this, smart home devices are often viewed as an always-there assistant.

Smart Homes have become very popular, according to Raju, R and Moh, M (2022) in the paper “Cyber Security in smart homes”, the last decade saw the point where internet devices surpassed the human population!

According to vxchnge.com, 30% of US homes now have either an Alexa or Google Nest smart speaker in them. Making these kind of devices a great target for hackers.
