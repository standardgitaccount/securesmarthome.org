Secure Smart Home

This is an all-in-one website giving you simple to understand, no-jargon information on how to secure your smart home.

Whilst Smart Homes are a great addition to everyday life, making mundane tasks easier, most people are unaware of the potential security risks. As well as this, it is not usually a main concern of the manufacturer. The aim of this site is to be a "hub" of information on how to mitigate these risks so people can enjoy the conveniences worry-free!

The site aims to help smart home users with their security, by breaking down smart home security into four jargon-free sections, easily accessible on the site.
